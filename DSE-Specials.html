DSE 9120 7,3 Amp 12V Switch

Mode Battery Charger

R 450.00 Excl. VAT*

  • Lightweight and compact design.
  • Designed for permanent connection to automotive batteries, keeping them charged to their maximum capacity.
  • Supply a standing load that is attached to a battery.
  • No moving parts, improved reliability.
  • Designed to avoid overcharging and overloading.
  • Continue to operate during cranking and running and have the ability to accept multiple AC voltage connections.
  • Supply current to a battery until the battery terminal voltage is equal to the set float voltage. After this it will change to a trickle charge current.

DSE4410 Auto Start Electronic

CANbus Control Module

R 1 850.00 Excl. VAT*
