Company Profile

Established in 1999, Hagar Distribution specialises in sales and technical support for the complete Deep Sea Electronics range . We pride ourselves with having good stock holdings of all of the popular Deep Sea Electronics units; therefore having a vast stock availability. We offer the complete package from sales inquiries, to electrical design, to on site commissioning and after sales technical support.

Situated in the industrial city of Germiston, (15kms east of Johannesburg), we are in the heart of the African sub-continent powerhouse and often pay visits to neighbouring countries (Angola, Botswana, Congo, Lesotho, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, Swaziland, Tanzania, Zambia & Zimbabwe). We also have technical support in Cape Town and in Durban.

Specialist Areas

  • DSE product supplier and technical expertise.
  • Emergency power panel manufacturer and specialist.
  • Electrical design with professional CAD generated drawings.
  • Fault finding, testing and commissioning.
  • Synchronising and load sharing specialist.
  • Engine harness manufacturers.
  • Power Factor Panel manufactures and specialists.
